# **App Name**: DreamWeave

## Core Features:

- Dream Capture: Record dreams using voice-to-text input with a visually appealing waveform display.
- AI Dream Weaving: Generate surreal art from dream journal entries using AI. The generated dream art has gold borders with neon red progress aura to indicate AI tool activity.
- NFT Marketplace: Allow users to mint and trade their dream-inspired NFTs.
- Profile Management: Display a user's dream streak, NFT collection, and energy meter.

## Style Guidelines:

- Dominant dark mode with deep matte black backgrounds (#000000 to #0F0F0F).
- Metallic gold accents (#D4AF37) for buttons, icons, and highlights provide a luxurious feel, like expensive jewelry.
- Vibrant neon red glows (#FF0033 to #FF1A4D) for active states, notifications, and dream energy pulses provide contrast.
- Premium typography using a sleek sans-serif font for all text elements; color is gold. 'Inter' sans-serif is recommended.
- Clean, minimal layout with generous negative space and subtle glow and shadow effects, contributes to surreal subconscious vibe.
- Use a stylized golden dreamcatcher with a single neon red thread knot at the center on a black background for the app icon, with a subtle outer glow.
- Employ subtle, dream-like animations throughout the app to enhance the cyber-luxe aesthetic. Dream energy pulses are neon red.
